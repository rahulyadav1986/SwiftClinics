Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who should get a Covid-19 test with Swift Clinics?

Covid-19 testing is available to:

  • passengers traveling internationally that require a negative test
  • individuals who do not need a diagnostic and would like to screen themselves for Covid-19

2. Who is eligible for our testing services?

Who is eligible for our testing services?

  • you have no new Covid-19 related symptoms (such as pink eye, rash, cough, etc.)
  • you have not been in contact with someone known to have Covid-19 within the past 14 days
  • you have not been advised by any public health agency or the Covid-alert App to get tested due to exposure

3. Are your tests accepted for travel purposes?

Yes, our tests are approved for international travel requiring a negative test. RT-PCR as well as Rapid Antigen tests are available. Passengers are responsible for confirming the testing requirements of their destination to select a suitable service.

4. How is a Covid-19 test performed?

Our covid-19 tests require a throat and/or a gentle nasal sample. We do not require a nasopharyngeal swab, however it is our preferred method to produce the most reliable results.

5. How do I know if your Covid-19 tests are accurate and reliable?

Swift Clinics only uses tests which have received Health Canada approval. Our PCR tests are fully FDA and WHO (World Health Organization) approved.

6. How long will the results take?

Our standard tests guarantee results in 6 hours. Our Urgent testing services guarantee results in 90 minutes,. In practice, the urgent service results may be available as soon as 30-60 minutes.
